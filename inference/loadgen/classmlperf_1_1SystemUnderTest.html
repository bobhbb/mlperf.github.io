virtual void mlperf::SystemUnderTest::FlushQueries ( )
pure virtual

Called immediately after the last call to IssueQuery in a series is made.

This doesn't necessarily signify the end of the test since there may be multiple series involved during a test; for example in accuracy mode. Clients can use this to flush any deferred queries immediately, rather than waiting for some timeout. This is especially useful in the server scenario.

Implemented in perf_tests::SystemUnderTestNullPool, perf_tests::SystemUnderTestNullStdAsync, unit_tests::SystemUnderTestBasic, and perf_tests::SystemUnderTestNull.

◆ IssueQuery()

virtual void mlperf::SystemUnderTest::IssueQuery ( const std::vector< QuerySample > &  samples)
pure virtual

Lets the loadgen issue N samples to the SUT.

The SUT may either a) return immediately and signal completion at a later time on another thread or b) it may block and signal completion on the current stack. The load generator will handle both cases properly. Note: The data for neighboring samples may or may not be contiguous depending on the scenario.
