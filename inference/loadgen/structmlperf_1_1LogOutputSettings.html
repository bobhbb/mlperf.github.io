Detailed Description

Specifies where log outputs should go.

By default, the loadgen outputs its log files to outdir and modifies the filenames of its logs with a prefix and suffix. Filenames will take the form: "<outdir>/<datetime><prefix>summary<suffix>.txt"

Affordances for outputing logs to stdout are also provided.
